## [Unreleased]

### Added
- **Export to PNG and PDF** — an "Export ▼" dropdown in the toolbar lets you download the full org chart as a high-resolution PNG (2× for retina displays) or a vector PDF. The export captures the entire tree regardless of the current pan/zoom level, inlines all CSS styles and colors, and embeds the DM Sans font when available. Powered by jsPDF 4.2.1 and svg2pdf.js 2.7.0.
